Bibliotheca Hagiographica Syriaca Electronica

Bibliotheca Hagiographica Syriaca Electronica describes texts written about saints and includes information about different versions and data such as the title, prologue, and epilogue of the saint's life, along with the hagiographer. The database notes if there are ancient or modern translations of the texts, the critical edition of the text, the MSS containing the Vita, and relevant secondary literature. This reference work is derived from the earlier Biblioteca Hagiographica Syriaca (BHS) project that was created by Fr. Ugo Zanetti and Dr. Claude Detienne but never published.

Bibliotheca Hagiographica Syriaca Electronica is a component part of Gateway to the Syriac Saints a linked digital resource for the study of hagiography. Gateway to the Syriac Saints links BHSE to Qadishe: A Guide to the Syriac Saints which contains information on persons relevant to the study of Syriac hagiography include the saints themselves (such as St. Simeon the Stylite) as well as their associates and the authors who narrated their lives or wrote homilies and hymns about them.

Bibliotheca Hagiographica Syriaca Electronica is also the first volume to be published in Syriaca.org's New Handbook of Syriac Literature.
