Welcome to The Syriac Gazetteer

Editors: Thomas A. Carlson (Princeton University) and David A. Michelson (Vanderbilt University)

The Syriac Gazetteer is a growing reference work concerning places relevant to Syriac Studies, currently containing articles about more than 2000 places.
