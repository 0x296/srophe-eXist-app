ATTCAT: The Annotation and Translation of Traditional Chinese Architecture Terminology Annual Research Forum

ATTCAT is a research forum for the inquiry into the meaning of technical terms in Traditional Chinese Architecture. 2018 was the initial meeting of the forum.
